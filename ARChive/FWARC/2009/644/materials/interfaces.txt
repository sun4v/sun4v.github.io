Exported Interfaces

Interface		Classification	Comments
=========		=============	========

assignable-path		Sun Private	Property in component PRI node,
					provides physical path of assignable
					unit (usually a PCI card).

rcid			Sun Private	Property in root-complex PRI node,
					provides an enumeration of RCs.

directio_capability	Sun Private	Property in Firmware node, indicates
					particular platform and firmware support
					IO virtualization.
